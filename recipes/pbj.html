PB&J Sandwich

Description

Peanut butter and jelly between two breads.

This is the good stuff.

Ingredients

  • 2 Breads
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Take one of the two breads.
  2. Put some jelly on the bread.
  3. Take the other, naked bread.
  4. Put some peanut butter on the bread.
  5. Slap the two breads together.
