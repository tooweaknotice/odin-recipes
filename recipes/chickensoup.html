Chicken Soup

Description

Some chicken soup.

Supposed to be good for the soul.

Ingredients

  • A whole precooked loss-leading chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water

Steps

  1. Shred the chicken up by hand and save the meat.
  2. Throw the picked carcass in boiling water.
  3. Boil for 30 minutes.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste, add in meat.
